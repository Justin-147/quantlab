# Backtest Assumptions

- Daily adjusted close prices are used for simulated market fills.
- Transaction cost and slippage are modeled as basis-point percentages of notional.
- Short selling, leverage, options, margin, and real broker execution are disabled.
- Portfolio weights may include inferred cash when target weights sum to less than one.
- Sample data is synthetic and should not be treated as real market history.
- Reported performance is a historical simulation and is not a forecast.
